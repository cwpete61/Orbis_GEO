# 🎬 Orbis Local Explainer Video Plan

### Creative Goal
A fast-paced, high-tech video with a **"neon glassmorphism"** aesthetic. It uses a dark navy background with glowing orange and blue accents to match the Orbis Local brand identity.

---

---

## 🎨 Creative Guidelines
*   **Tone**: Authoritative, cutting-edge, and urgent.
*   **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (Landscape)
*   **Pacing**: Upbeat, dynamic, transitioning every 5-10 seconds.
*   **Music**: Pulsing, futuristic synth-wave that builds tension and resolves into a confident rhythm.
*   **Voice Style**: Professional, authoritative tech-narrator with crisp articulation.
*   **Color Palette**:
    *   **Primary Background**: `#130f40` (Dark Navy)
    *   **Accents**: `#f0932b` (Orange), `#686de0` (Purple/Blue), `#2ecc71` (Neon Green).

---

## 🤖 Instructions for AI Video Generators (Synthesia/HeyGen)
1. **Avatar Selection**: Choose a sleek, "Innovation Expert" or "Futurist" persona.
2. **Environment**: Use a glassmorphic background with data overlays.
3. **Pacing**: Set script timing to be energetic and avoid long pauses.
