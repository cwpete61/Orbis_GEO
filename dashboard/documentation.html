Introduction: The Shift from SEO to GEO

AI search is eating traditional search. This means old strategies won't cut it anymore.

The Paradigm Shift

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews process information differently than traditional keyword-based crawlers. They synthesize context rather than just counting keyword density.

What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) focuses on writing highly citable, fact-dense content that LLMs want to fetch and present to users natively.

Deep Dive: AI Citability & llms.txt

AI bots don't want to parse your entire marketing page to find one fact. They want discrete, packaged knowledge.

The Anatomy of a Citable Passage

  • Length: 134-167 words is the sweet spot.
  • Self-Contained: The paragraph must make sense entirely on its own, without surrounding context.
  • Fact-Rich: High density of entities, numbers, names, and concrete data points.

The llms.txt Standard

The llms.txt file is rapidly becoming the new robots.txt standard for the Generative AI era. It explicitly tells crawlers exactly how to map your knowledge base optimally for model inclusion.

AI Crawler Security

Many legacy firewall settings actively block bots like GPTBot, ClaudeBot, and PerplexityBot. If they cannot access the DOM, you do not exist in their next training run.
